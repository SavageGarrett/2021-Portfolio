Garrett Carder About

I am the Student Manager of The Innovation Studio, CTO of See3D, 3D Printing for the Blind, and the Web Master for BDAA. I am also a self-taught full stack web developer.

My love for technology began at a young age with my grandfather teaching me how to use a drill. This love grew in to a burning passion to learn anything I could. I came from taking apart nerf guns as a kid, to now being able to run any tool at the makerspace I am the student manager at and developing on several different technology stacks.
